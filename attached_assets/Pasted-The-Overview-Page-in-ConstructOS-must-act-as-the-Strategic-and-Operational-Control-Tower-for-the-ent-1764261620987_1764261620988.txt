Dashboard Design PrinciplesThe layout should follow the F-Pattern or Z-Pattern of reading, placing the most critical, high-level metrics in the top-left area.Visual Hierarchy: Use large, bold numbers at the top (Level 1 data) for the absolute key metrics. Requires monitoring.Green: On Track/Good.Drill-Down: Every major KPI tile or chart must be clickable, leading directly to the relevant, filtered Projects Page or detailed report (e.g., clicking on "5 At-Risk Projects" takes you to the Project List filtered to those 5 projects).2. üìä Key Performance Indicator (KPI) SectionsThe Overview Page should be split into distinct, card-based sections, drawing data from all your microservices.A.
